ABOUT PROJECT

Timetable management sytem project has been developed using PHP,CSS.Boootstrap and Javascript.It has two users, an admin (head of a department and a student). The admin does the major operations i.e: inputs classes, lecturers,time ,courses which are among the inputs required for automatic generation of the timetable. The student can sign up and log in to view timetable.
