About timetable system

Timetable management sytem project has been developed using
Java,CSS and Javascript.It has two users, an admin
(head of a department and a student). The admin does the major operations
i.e: inputs classes, lecturers,time ,courses which are among the inputs
required for automatic generation of the timetable. Genetic algorithm
has been implimented for this automatic generation.
The student can sign up and log in to view timetable.
